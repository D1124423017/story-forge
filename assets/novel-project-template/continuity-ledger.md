## After-Chapter Update Protocol

After drafting each chapter, update:

1. Timeline and location.
2. Character knowledge, secrets, trust, debts, attraction, resentment, and status.
3. Injuries, fatigue, illness, resources, and equipment.
4. New clues, false leads, mysteries, and foreshadowing.
5. World rules used or changed.
6. Contradictions that must be repaired before the next chapter.
